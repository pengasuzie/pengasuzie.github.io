How It Works

Real data, honest confidence ratings

The skill reads your existing opportunity brief for context, then runs targeted research based on your priorities. It uses live web search to find real data — and is upfront about what it could and couldn't verify.

  • Reads existing opportunity brief for context
  • You choose research priorities (sizing, demand, trends, spending)
  • Live web search for real data and signals
  • Bottom-up market sizing (not top-down guesses)
  • Triangulates from multiple sources
  • Honest about uncertainty — flags low-confidence numbers

Research Principles
01

Be Honest

"I couldn't find reliable data" is more useful than a made-up number.

02

Triangulate

One source is an anecdote. Three sources pointing the same direction is a signal.

03

Demand, Not Hype

Press coverage doesn't equal market demand. Look for spending and search signals.

04

Bottom-Up

"10,000 companies × $500/mo = $60M" beats "the AI market is $200B".

05

Cite Sources

Always include where you found data so it can be verified.

Demand Signals

Where to look for real demand

Market size numbers are useful, but demand signals tell you whether people are actively looking for a solution right now. The skill tracks multiple signal types and rates their strength.

  • Google Trends — search volume and trajectory
  • Reddit/forum activity — people asking for solutions
  • Job postings — companies hiring for this problem
  • Startup funding — investor money flowing in
  • Conference activity — talks, tracks, and dedicated events
