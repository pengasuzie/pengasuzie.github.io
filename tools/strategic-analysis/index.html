How It Works

Grounded in what you actually know

Before touching the transcript, the skill gathers ALL available context: CLAUDE.md, prior conversations, competitor docs, git log, and strategic notes. Every assessment references actual product capabilities, not generic advice.

  • Reads CLAUDE.md, docs, git history, competitor files
  • Identifies participants and roles from transcript
  • Cross-references mentioned competitors with existing intel
  • Produces analysis.md (written) + analysis-voice.txt (TTS-ready)
  • Voice text optimized for natural spoken delivery
  • Works standalone or chained with voice-to-text and text-to-voice

Four Analysis Modes
01

Sales Deal

Win probability, feature-by-feature product fit against roadmap, competitive threats, development velocity signal, and prioritized next actions to close.

02

Consulting

What the client actually needs vs. what they said, AI opportunity map, recommended engagement approach, phased roadmap, and session preparation.

03

Competitive Intel

Extract competitive intelligence from conversations. Cross-reference against existing competitor docs. Market signals, pricing hints, positioning implications.

04

Meeting Debrief

Clean factual summary: decisions made, action items, commitments, open questions, key quotes. No strategic overlay — just the facts.

What Gets Produced

Two outputs, two purposes

Every analysis produces two files. The written analysis is a full markdown document with sections, evidence, and recommendations. The voice text is a stripped-down version optimized for text-to-speech — no markdown syntax, acronyms expanded, spoken cues added for natural pacing.

The voice text is designed to be piped directly into /text-to-voice to produce an MP3 briefing you can listen to on the go.
