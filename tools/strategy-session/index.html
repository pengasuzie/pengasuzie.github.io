How It Works

A conversation, not a framework dump

The skill thinks WITH you, not AT you. Part provocateur, part strategist, part devil's advocate. It reads all existing research, then pushes your thinking beyond the obvious with the right framework for what you need.

  • Reads opportunity brief, research, and competitor data
  • You choose the session type (7 frameworks)
  • Interactive back-and-forth, not a report dump
  • Challenges assumptions and pushes for specificity
  • Captures key insights, decisions, and next steps
  • Saves session notes to your opportunity directory

Seven Frameworks

Different thinking for different needs

Each framework is designed for a specific type of strategic question. Some sessions need structured analysis. Others need creative provocation. Pick the one that matches where you're stuck — or let the skill recommend one based on your context.

Every framework is interactive. The skill asks questions, challenges your answers, and pushes you to think deeper rather than just generating a report you'll never read.

Framework When To Use
Go-to-Market Which segment first? What channel? What wedge gets you in the door?
Business Model SaaS, marketplace, productised service, licensing, freemium, pay-per-use?
Differentiation Cost leadership, unique approach, niche focus, or new category?
Blue Ocean Eliminate, reduce, raise, create — redesign the value curve.
Problem Reframe Are you solving the right problem? For the right customer?
Risk Mitigation Name it, rate it, mitigate it. Build a risk matrix with contingencies.
Open Brainstorm What if it were easy? What would it look like with $5K and 30 days?
What Gets Captured

Every session ends with structured capture

No matter which framework you choose, the session always ends the same way — with a structured summary of insights, ideas, decisions, open questions, and action items. Everything saved to your opportunity directory so nothing gets lost.

The capture format is consistent across all seven frameworks, making it easy to review past sessions and track how your thinking evolved over time.
